Manual: Using the Applicaion

Demonstration for using the Application

NARMADA for Covid is based on our research work which was presented in ACL 2020 Social NLP workshop (aclweb.org/anthology/2020.socialnlp-1.3). Narmada is a resource matching assistant for resources needed during a disaster.

Find tweets: Use this option to find tweets specific to the requirement. Enter resource words (such as oxygen, beds etc), and a location. Specify you need to resource, or you have the resource and are looking to share it with others.

All tweets: View all recent tweets in India related to covid, classified into two bins. Click on a tweet to view the corresponding matched tweets.

By and large, the system scrapes latest tweets, segregates them into "Need" and "Available" categories and helps create matches among them. Have a look at the demo video to learn about how it works!
